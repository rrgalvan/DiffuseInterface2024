Overview

The 9th European Congress of Mathematics (9ECM, https://www.ecm2024sevilla.com) will be held in Seville from 15 to 19 July 2024. Our team, formed by Francisco Guillén-González, J. Rafael Rodríguez-Galván and Daniel Acosta-Soba, has been interested for years on numerical analysis and simulation of Phase Filed and Diffuse Interface models, oriented to applications to physics and biology. Recently, our research has focused on modelling of tumor growth using this kind of models. In this regard, we are preparing a 9ECM-minisymposium proposal focused on "Diffuse Interface Models in Physics and Biology".
